Modern Urban Apartment

A transformation of a dated duplex into a bright, open-concept living space with custom storage solutions and smart home technology in New York's Upper West Side.

Project Details

This 1,800 sq ft apartment was completely redesigned to create a cohesive flow between living spaces while maximizing natural light. We removed several walls to create an open-concept living/dining/kitchen area and added custom built-ins throughout for efficient storage.

The color palette features warm neutrals with bold accents in the accessories and artwork. Materials were selected for durability and timeless appeal, including white oak flooring, quartz countertops, and matte black hardware.

Smart home features include automated lighting, motorized shades, and a whole-home audio system that can be controlled via smartphone.
